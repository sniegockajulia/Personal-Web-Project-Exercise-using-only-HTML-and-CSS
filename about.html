About the Author

My story, my inspirations, and my path to becoming an author.

A Passion for Storytelling

Hello! I’m Julia Śniegocka, an author who has been writing for 10 years. I started my journey as a writer with a love for classic literature. Over the years, I’ve published 6 novels and countless short stories. My work is a blend of romance and mystery, with characters and plots that reflect the beauty and complexity of life.

Books That Changed Me

Here are a few books that have shaped my writing style and creativity:

  • The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald – For its lyrical prose and vivid imagery.
  • To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee – A story of courage and moral growth.
  • 1984 by George Orwell – For its exploration of society, power, and truth.
